Where

Have you checked your Location settings?   Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat may be collecting information that can reveal your location even if you opted out from the location data collection. For location information, multiple information is collected, ranging from information collected from your data like GPS, wireless networks, cell towers, Wi-Fi access points, and other sensors, such as gyroscopes, accelerometers, and compasses. However, your location can be easily tracked using other information that is collected without your consent. On of the methods can be zeroing down on your location using nearby Wifi access points, which Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat collects without your consent, under Device Information.   

What

We’re pretty sure that you’ve heard of cookies…. But have you heard of Web Beacons, Web Storage, or Pixels Tags??  Many companies that collects your information utilizes cookies to collect your data. This information is provided through Cookie Policy. However, not all companies are nice enough to tell you how they collect their data. In fact, cookies are just one method of collecting your data. Web beacons, web storage, and pixels can be utilized to collect your information without you knowing it, unless you look into your website and application deeply. Just to give you a brief idea what these other methods can do, companies can collect all of your input (including what you write using your keyboard and what you click using your mouse) and know which websites you go into. Snapchat, Facebook, and Instagram defines their ‘cookies’ in Cookie Policy in different ways. Some define web beacons, web storage, and pixel tags in different ways, and NONE of the three companies specifically inform users which of the data collection methods exists, and what information are collected through them.

Why

The reason why we are informing you about these issues is because we believe that user information and user privacy is very important and there are not many people who are aware of these issues. All information that the users receive by the company is through documentations like Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy. And yet more than 95% of the users do not read or go through the documentations before using services like Snapchat, Instagram, and Facebook. We saw it crucial to make more and more people to be aware of these issues, so that service providers will pay more attention to these issues and clarify what are not well-clarified with the given documentations of the status quo.
